ImageTools Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026‑05‑27

This policy explains what data ImageTools collects, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights. ImageTools is operated by Aspedia Pty Ltd. Contact: philg@aspedia.net.

What we collect

  • Images you upload — via the web interface, mobile companion app, or the ImageTools Screenshot Capture browser extension. We store these against your user account so you can edit, compress, and share them.
  • Image metadata (EXIF) — extracted from uploaded images. Used for organisation and editing features (e.g. capture date, GPS, camera).
  • Account information — username, display name, and email address obtained from your authenticated session (OAuth2 with vinCreative, or Authelia / Remote‑User headers, depending on deployment).
  • Service logs — request paths, IP address, and timestamps for security, rate‑limiting, and debugging. Retained up to 90 days.

What we don’t collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not collect search terms.
  • The browser extension only captures images when you explicitly invoke it (popup button, context menu, or keyboard shortcut). It does not run in the background and does not collect telemetry.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertising or analytics third parties.

How long we keep data

  • Images uploaded by authenticated users are kept until you delete them or your account is deactivated.
  • Images uploaded anonymously (no signed‑in account) are deleted after 30 days.
  • Service logs are retained for up to 90 days.

Where data is stored

All images, metadata, and account records are stored on infrastructure operated by Aspedia Pty Ltd. Your browser extension stores its authentication token in browser.storage on your device; that token is sent only to the ImageTools backend you connected it to, over HTTPS.

Third‑party processors

ImageTools optionally integrates with OpenRouter when its AI features are enabled. When you explicitly invoke an AI action, the relevant image and prompt are sent to OpenRouter, which forwards them to the underlying model provider you selected. No data is sent to AI providers unless you initiate an AI feature.

Your rights

  • You can delete any image at any time from the web UI.
  • You can revoke OAuth2 sessions and connected browser extensions from the Settings panel.
  • To request account deletion or a copy of your data, email philg@aspedia.net.

Browser extension specifics

ImageTools Screenshot Capture captures images only when you explicitly invoke a capture (visible area, full page, or selection). Captures are sent over HTTPS to your authenticated ImageTools backend. No third party receives these images.

The extension requests activeTab, contextMenus, storage, notifications, tabs, and host permission for <all_urls>. The host permission is required so the capture works on every page; it does not cause the extension to read or send page content automatically.

Changes to this policy

We will update this page if we change how data is collected or used. The Effective date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
